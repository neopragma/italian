Modi e tempi del verbo

Modi verbali Tempi verbali
Indicativo
Presente (Present) io lavo i piatti I wash the dishes (right now or regularly)
? (Present Progressive) io sto lavando i piatti I'm washing the dishes (right now)
Passato prossimo (Present Perfect) io ho lavato i piatti I have washed the dishes (and they were clean the last time I saw them)
Imperfetto (Imperfect) io lavavo i piatti I washed or was washing or used to wash the dishes (at some point in the past)
Trapassato prossimo (Past Perfect) io avevo lavato i piatti I had been washing the dishes (and then something funny happened)
Passato remoto (Remote Past) io lavai i piatti I washed the dishes (???)
Futuro semplice (Simple Future) io laverò i piatti I will wash the dishes
Futuro anteriore (Future Perfect) io avrò lavato i piatti I will have washed the dishes
Congiuntivo
Presente (Present) (non vale la pena) che io lavi i piatti (it's not worth it) that I'm washing the dishes
Passato (Past) (non vale la pena) che io abbia lavato i piatti (it's not worth it) that I washed the dishes
Imperfetto (Imperfect) (non vale la pena) che io lavassi i piatti (it's not worth it) that I wash the dishes (???)
Trapassato (Past Perfect) (può darsi) che io avassi lavato i piatti (it's possible) that I had washed the dishes
Condizionale
Presente (Present) io laverei i piatti I would wash the dishes
Passato (Past) io avrei lavato I would have washed the dishes
Imperativo (Imperative)
Presente (Present) lava i piatti! (Hey self): Wash the dishes!
Infinito (Infinitive)
Presente (Present) lavare i piatti to wash the dishes
Passato (Past) avere lavato i piatti having washed the dishes
Participio (Participle)
Presente (Present) lavante i piatti washing the dishes
Passato (Past) lavato i piatti I washed the dishes
Gerundio (Gerund)
Presente (Present) lavando i piatti washing the dishes
Passato (Past) avendo lavato i piatti having washed the dishes
